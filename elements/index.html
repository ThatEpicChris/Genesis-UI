Design

Minimalism gives the view of a clutterless, easy, and simple envornment. But sometimes Minimalism can go too far, and strip every thing away from the actual design. This is where Genesis comes in, keeping a certain aspect of minimalism, but adding a colorful interface to create a colorful, appealing, yet simple UI.
